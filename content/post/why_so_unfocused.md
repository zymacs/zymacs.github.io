If you  look around my blog you will see a lot of articles. Not so many
but year, many in some sense.

The problem.

Many topics. Its hard to tell what I am into. If you asked me, I would't
really have a ready made answer. Actually, now that I think of what
answer I'd have for the question, one comes to mind: I want it all.

But then I have a "Hire Me" button in the footer. "Hire Me" for...


I guess what am trying to say is I'm making a bit of a change. Or, let's say,
attempting to. I do have a lot of interests and IT is wide. All topics
seem fun to dive into.  But, without a plan, without a goal to run towards, without some solid signal this is who I am on this site, this won't be of any gain
to anyone. To me.

So a rough plan. I had uploaded a list of books I was reading. I will cut that
down to two. Git and CPP.

I will also be making sure to not write about anything not related to what my
primary reading covers. I do alot of reading but I guess I should be selective
about what makes it to the site. Most of it can remain as personal
notes and come to the site when there is purpose for it.

Say I have a lot of drafts on memory, ram,  cpus. I can dump all these out when
I set out on a project that needs them all for prerequisites. I guess that'll be
easier on my brain than what I have now. It's pulling away at the brain
from multiple directions.

So recap

- Git
- CPP
- Zhou Finance
- Last Week In Code

This should last at least as long as .. Let's try one week.
